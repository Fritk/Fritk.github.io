Welcome to Levina's Bakery

We are an award-winning bakery located in McCook, Nebraska. At Levina's Bakery our passion is to produce breathtaking and delicious homemade cinnamon rolls, pasteries and custom cakes that will not only compliment your special events but will be remembered for years to come.

Come and enjoy the relaxed atmospher of one of McCook's premier meeting places for informal buisness meetings or just a cup of coffee with an old friend. We also offer a variety of items that can be custom made, so keep us in mind for your next themed event.
